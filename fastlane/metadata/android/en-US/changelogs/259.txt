NewsBlur 14 is a ground-up redesign of the Android app.

- Full edge-to-edge UI for Android 15
- Story highlights: save text in stories
- Custom folder and feed icons
- Full text intelligence classifiers
- User tags on saved stories
- Home screen widget with synced stories
- Code block rendering in stories
- Improved embedded video display
- Predictive back gestures (Android 14+)
- Self-hosted certificate support
- Rewritten in Kotlin with Jetpack Compose
- Faster sync and database performance
